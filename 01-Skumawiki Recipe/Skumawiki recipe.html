Skumawiki Recipe

Join me today as we will be cooking a delicious skumawiki together

Ingredients

  • Cooking oil
  • Onions
  • Royco
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Chop the onions into small slices
  2. Add the choped onions into a heated cooking oil in the sufuria
  3. Wait for the onion to turn golden brown
  4. Add the chopped skumawiki
  5. let it cook for atleast 3min then Stirring
  6. Springle Royco mchuzi mix
  7. Add salt to taste
  8. Serve while still hot
